Review Generator Released - 22 Sep 2016

The initial version of the IndiTools Review Generator has been released. This tools allows you to enter the urls of your reviews on Independent Girls and create a nicely formatted review excerpt block that you can cut and paste into your advert, signature or profile.

Review Blocks are a great way to communicate those things you aren't strictly allowed to mention in your ads, but which are allowed in reviews. There a great marketing tool, especially for newbie IndiGirls just starting out, who need to work particularly hard at establishing your reputation.

But, reviews work equally well for Legends, there's nothing like pointing to your latest reviews to give everyone a reminder of how you got to be as Legendary as you are. A great little boost to your business during off-season never hurt anyone!
